OLIG2

OLIG2 specifies neural progenitors into oligodendrocyte progenitor cells and the oligodendroglial lineage [1-4]. OLIG2 promotes OPC differentiation into myelin-forming oligodendrocytes and supports myelination [1,3,4]. OLIG2 is required for oligodendrocyte development [2,4]. With partial Shh inhibition, Olig2 overexpression in human iPSCs drove oligodendroglial specification by upregulating oligodendroglial markers and increasing NG2+ cells [1]. In tMCAO rats, transplanted Olig2-OPCs partly differentiated into MBP+ oligodendrocytes and engrafted better [1]. The transplanted cells promoted myelination and improved cognition [1]. OLIG2 is required for oligodendrocyte development and binds coding and lncRNA genes [2]. OLIG2 can activate or repress lncRNAs and represses lnc-OPC in neural stem cells [2]. Olig2 knockout prevents oligodendroglial lineage and Olig2 overexpression promotes OPC and oligodendrocyte generation [2]. Olig2 is a bHLH transcription factor expressed in neural progenitors that specifies them into motor neurons and OPCs [3]. Olig2 primes OPCs for differentiation into myelin-forming oligodendrocytes [3]. Olig2-E1 physically interacts with OLIG2 in oligodendrocytes and CRISPRi silencing of Olig2-E1 downregulated Olig2 [3]. Olig2 is a key transcription factor in oligodendrocyte differentiation [4]. Olig2+ cells are specified in the preoptic area and initially coexpress Sox2 [4]. Olig2+ cells then colocalize with sox10 and express mbpa while ensheathing the optic nerve [4]. Mesencephalic olig2/sox10 oligodendrocytes differentiate earlier [4]. In Opalin cKO mice, the percentage of CC1+ mature oligodendrocytes among Olig2+ cells in the corpus callosum was reduced [5]. In Opalin icKO mice, the ratio of CC1+/Olig2+ cells to Olig2+ cells in the corpus callosum was significantly decreased [5].
