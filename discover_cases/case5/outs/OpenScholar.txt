## MYOD1 — muscle cells, muscle fibers, or myocytes

**Query:** What functional relationships between MYOD1 and muscle cells, muscle fibers, or myocytes have been directly reported in peer-reviewed research articles?

MYOD1 has also been shown to inhibit the expression of FUT4, a gene involved in glycosphingolipid biosynthesis, thereby suppressing cell migration and invasion [3]. In addition, MYOD1 has been found to be repressed in cells with cardiolipin deficiency, leading to impaired muscle differentiation [4]. Furthermore, MYOD1 has a role in regulating CCND2 expression, which is involved in muscle formation and differentiation in cattle [8]. Furthermore, MYOD1 has been shown to have a role in regulating the expression of other genes involved in muscle development, such as PDE1B, ADAMTS1, DPT, and CCND2 [8]. The expression of MYOD1 is also influenced by other factors, such as fibroblast growth factor (FGF), which can inhibit MYOD1 expression and prevent myogenic differentiation [5]. Overall, the findings from these studies highlight the complex regulatory mechanisms involved in MYOD1 expression and function in muscle cells and fibers.
