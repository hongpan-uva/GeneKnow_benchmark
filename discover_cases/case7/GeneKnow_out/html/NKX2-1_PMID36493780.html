Our scRNA-seq data ( Figures 1A and 1B ) suggested that the late-stage tip cells gain alveolar differentiation competence. We therefore tested whether the Lin POS organoids could differentiate into AT2 cells. In medium containing DAPT (γ-secretase inhibitor; Notch inhibitior), DCI (dexamethasone, cyclic AMP [cAMP], and 3-Isobutyl-1-methylxanthine [IBMX]), CHIR (CHIR99021; Wnt agonist) and SB431542 (TGF-β inhibition), NKX2-1, SFTPC , and ACE2 were upregulated and SOX9, SOX2 , and TP63 downregulated ( Figures 3A and S3A–S3C ).
